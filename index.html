Who We Are

Pioneering Agro-Exporters

Based in the technological hub of Hyderabad, Shukr Global is a premier agro-export company specializing in premium-quality dehydrated artisanal powders. We act as a trusted global bridge between fertile, certified artisanal farms in India and international food processors, seasoning packagers, and culinary giants.

Our commitment is rooted in absolute transparency, grade-A moisture control, and compliance with strict food safety frameworks of the USA, Europe, and the Middle East.

Farm-to-Port Traceability

We trace every batch back to artisanal farms, maintaining full compliance with clean label requirements.

Advanced Moisture Management

Processed under low temperatures (<60°C) to keep moisture levels strictly below 4.0%, preventing clumping and preserving active flavors.

How We Do It

The Export & Processing Cycle

From crop selection to global ports, our dehydration process ensures the natural qualities of the produce are perfectly preserved.

01

Strict Artisanal Sourcing

We source raw onions and tomatoes directly from certified artisanal farms, screening out chemical traces or heavy metals.

02

Low-Temp Dehydration

Advanced conveyor dehydrators maintain uniform airflow at temperatures below 60°C to lock in original taste, enzymes, and visual color.

03

Aseptic Packaging & Shipping

Products are sealed in food-grade aluminum bags or polythene-lined corrugated cartons to ensure zero moisture ingress during transit.
